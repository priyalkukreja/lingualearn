FAQ

Common questions from parents & students

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is LinguaLearn free to use? +
Yes! You get a 30-day free trial with full access to all features — AI tutor, mock tests, voice lab, everything. No credit card needed. After the trial, we have affordable plans starting at ₹99/month.
Which languages and classes are supported? +
We support 8 CBSE languages — French, German, Japanese, Spanish, Sanskrit, Korean, Mandarin, and Russian — for Classes 6 through 10. All content is aligned to the latest CBSE syllabus and textbooks.
How does the AI tutor work? +
Our AI tutor "Gyani" understands your class level and textbook. You can ask any question in Hindi or English, and Gyani explains it simply with examples. It's like having a private tutor available 24/7.
Are the mock tests based on actual CBSE patterns? +
Absolutely. Our mock tests follow exact CBSE exam patterns — Unit Tests (40 min), Half-Yearly (90 min), and Annual exams (3 hours). Questions are designed to match the difficulty and format of real CBSE papers.
Can my child use this on a phone? +
Yes! LinguaLearn works beautifully on phones, tablets, and computers. No app download needed — just open padhlo.life in any browser and start learning.
How quickly will I see results? +
Most students see a 15-25% improvement within the first term. Our weakness engine identifies gaps early and targets them with drills, so you improve faster than traditional studying.
